Website Strategy Catalog

Find the right website approach for your audience and goals

Different businesses need different websites. This catalog breaks down how to think about website strategy by industry — who your audience is, what they need to feel, and what action you want them to take.

This is not a template library

Most website advice focuses on design and technology. Pick a theme. Choose your colors. Launch it.

But the real problem is earlier than that. Most businesses don't stop to think about who they are building for, what that person needs to feel before they take action, or what's getting in their way.

This catalog is about that thinking. Every industry here has a different audience with different fears, questions, and needs. A cleaning company's website works completely differently from a ministry's. A contractor's site requires different trust signals than a chiropractor's.

When you understand those differences — before you touch a single design tool — you build something that actually works.

"The goal is not just to have a website."

The goal is to help the right people understand, trust, and take the next step.
